Most importantly, coronaviruses are zoonotic, meaning they are transmitted between animals and people. People are told to be cautious about having signs of infection include respiratory symptoms, fever, cough, shortness of breath and breathing difficulties. In more severe cases, infection can cause pneumonia, severe acute respiratory syndrome, kidney failure and even death. Governments and organizations have given out recommendations to prevent infection spread include regular hand washing, covering mouth and nose when coughing and sneezing, thoroughly cooking meat and eggs. Avoid close contact with anyone showing symptoms of respiratory illness such as coughing and sneezing.

In this outbreak, many people were unable to stay at home. They must stick to their posts to maintain the operation of the city and the normal life of the citizens. These groups include medical staff, scientific researchers, police security personnel, as well as transport drivers, courier, and cleaning staff. While ordinary people staying at home because of the lockdown are also trying to maintain confidence and passion for life. They are bringing happiness and comfort to their families and neighbors, and they are also worthy of respect
